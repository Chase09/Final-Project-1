About Us


We offer free advance diagnostic to all storage media. Just bring your storage media (hard drives, flash drives, SD cards, etc.) to our shop and we will employ all necessary diagnostic procedures, from PCB component diagnostics, PCB firmware diagnostic, Service Area diagnostic, platter surface and head crack inspection inside our Class 5 Clean Room. After the diagnostic procedures we will let you know the cost of recovering the data, and if you do not wish to proceed, there is no charge and you may simply have your disk back. You've got nothing to lose - we are here to help you!
